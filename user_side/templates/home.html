Lapu-Lapu City, located in the heart of Cebu, is a must-visit destination for history buffs and beach lovers alike. Whether you're exploring the historic Mactan Shrine, diving in crystal-clear waters, or savoring delicious seafood, Lapu-Lapu City has something for everyone.
